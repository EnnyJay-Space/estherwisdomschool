About Dr Esther Fubara-ManuelF

Dr Esther Rex Fubara-Manuel is an Apostle with a vision to "Glorify Divinity And Bless Humanity". She is the Chancellor of Esther's Wisdom Academy ( EWA). She is a teacher by profession and a preacher by occupation.

The word WISDOM in EWA is an acronym meaning: Women Of Impact School For Development Of Ministries. The School which was formally known as Price Of Virtue School Of Ministry (POVSOM) was founded thirty years ago and graduated forty eight young ladies and two young men. Many of the graduates are currently happily married mothers and successful ministers of the gospel, within and outside Nigeria.

Through the years Dr Esther Rex has established structures and ministries that can showcase the fulfillment of the twelve - fold ministries of POVSOM( rebranded as EWA), just like the victorious woman with a crown of twelve stars( ministries), in Revelation 12. Those ministries are related to the twelve courses in EWA.

PROFITABILITY OF E.W.A

On a general note, the Mission SSS: Great SOLUTIONS and Good SUCCESS for God's SERVICE spells out the following benefits of EWI

PRACTICAL SOLUTIONS

EWA offers practical teaching, mentorship, and Q&A sessions to address challenges that hinder women from living fully and fulfilling their purpose.

NETWORKING & MENTORING

EWA connects students with mentors and professionals, building their capacity for success in life, relationships, business and ministry.
